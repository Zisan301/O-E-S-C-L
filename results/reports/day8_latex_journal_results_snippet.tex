\subsection{C/S/C+S Band Comparison}
Day-8 extends the focused C-band PCS confirmation to C, S, and combined C+S scenarios. The strongest scenario is C+S, with $\nu=0.36$, 12 spans, and 0.0 dBm/channel. It gives a paired GMI gain of 0.0732$\pm$0.0118 bits/symbol and a rate gain of 0.0078$\pm$0.0013 Tb/s/channel. The C+S case uses a simplified lumped inter-band penalty and is not claimed as full Raman-calibrated WDM validation.
