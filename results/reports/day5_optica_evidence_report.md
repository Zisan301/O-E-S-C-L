# Day-5 Optica Evidence Report

## What Day-5 adds

- Waveform-level SSFM starter with RRC pulse shaping and matched filtering.
- Receiver chromatic-dispersion compensation.
- Memory-aware neural equalizer using neighboring symbols.
- Exact bit-metric AWGN-likelihood GMI/NGMI.
- Stress selector to avoid saturated or random operating regions.
- Acceptance gate against Raw, Linear EQ, DBP-like, and Polynomial NLC baselines.
