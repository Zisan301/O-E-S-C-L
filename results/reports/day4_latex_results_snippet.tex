\subsection{SSFM-Lite Validation and Bit-Metric GMI}
Day-4 replaces the earlier analytical screening-only model with an SSFM-lite propagation model and evaluates bit-metric AWGN-likelihood GMI/NGMI. The best operating point is obtained by PCS + Neural at 6 spans, 2.0 dBm/channel, band C, and $\nu=0.24$. The method obtains GMI=0.003$\pm$0.001 bits/symbol, NGMI=0.001$\pm$0.000, BER=4.668e-01, and GSNR=0.01$\pm$0.01 dB.
