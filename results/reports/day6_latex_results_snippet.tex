\subsection{Day-6 Focused PCS Confirmation}
A focused Day-6 confirmation was performed at the Day-5B operating region: C band, 10 spans, 4.0 dBm/channel, and evidence stress. Using 7 seeds and 8192 symbols/seed, the best nonzero PCS coefficient was $\nu=0.32$. Compared with uniform signaling, PCS improved bit-metric GMI by 0.0284$\pm$0.0173 bits/symbol and estimated rate by 0.0030$\pm$0.0018 Tb/s/channel. The corresponding PCS operating point achieved GMI=3.2113, NGMI=0.8028, and BER=4.569e-02; the uniform reference achieved GMI=3.1829, NGMI=0.7957, and BER=4.863e-02. This supports a PCS-centered operating-region claim; neural superiority is not claimed.
